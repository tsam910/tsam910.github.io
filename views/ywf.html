Young Wild Free
Designer
2015

Young Wild Free cafe was started by Amit, a friend I had worked with earlier. I was reluctant to take up a freelancing project at the time, but he couldn't find any other designer in time and I thought it would be interesting to work on.

The cafe is themed around music. The branding had to reflect that, and yet not go overboard with it. I did the following things for Young Wild Free

  • Logo
  • Promotional posters
  • Menu
  • Other graphics like social media assets, visiting cards etc.

This is the logo we started with. I was into hand lettering those days and thought that style would suit the brand too, so I drew it by hand.
 
This was a word mark I later made because it had the name in full. It seemed to go better will all graphics so we decided to use this as the logo.
A page of the menu. This gets folded in half, so this is the left and the right page together. We decided to have a song and some photographs on each page because it keeps the user engaged, and it worked out really well. We got a lot of word of mouth marketing because of our unique menu design.
This is a promotional poster. We put out a series of posters like this, with different hashtags and background images. This was a very low cost marketing move that worked well.
