Vivah Place
UI/UX Designer, Front-end developer
2013-14

Vivah Place was the brainchild of one of my college friends. I liked the idea and thought it had potential. He, me and another friend worked on it for a few months and were about to launch an alpha when we ran into a revenue generation problem. From there, we decided to not pursue it any further because we didn't have the resources we'd have to put in initially to get any return later.

I designed the UI/UX and coded the front end in this project.
