Restaurant Mobile App - Realbox - 2013

An important lesson I learned while working with Realbox is that people aren't looking out for products, they're looking out for solutions. While we were talking to customers and potential customers about our POS and Interactive Menu systems, we realized that they also had other problems and ambitions. For example, they wanted to be active and seen on social media, they wanted to have a good interactive website, they wanted a mobile application etc. Seeing this, rather than selling individual applications, we deicded to create packages of applications. Some of the apps in these packages would be paid, some others would be free. We developed a mobile application for restaurants as a part of the package

For different restaurants, the mobile application would be very similar. The structure of the app would have menu, home page, address, offers and other things, and only the content and the look would be different. So, for scalability, we made a signle adaptable mobile application. The most important feature of the application was its customizability. All the data, including the theme, the menu, the address etc, is stored in a single file. To change the whole look and content of the application, one only has to make changes to one data file.
