Desconto - President of Design & Media (2012)

Desconto is a discount card started by some students of DU in 2012. Desconto was my first project as a graphic designer and I was very excited about it. When I first got a call and was asked if I was a designer, I shamelessly said yes. I actually was a front end developer with a very keen interest in design. It has always worked to my favor that most non-tech people can't properly distinguish between front-end, User Interface and design. Anyway, I was asked to send a few samples of my work and design something as a screening test. Fortunately, out of the designs they were screening, they liked mine the most and a couple of meetings later, I was the President of Design and Media for Desconto.

My role there was to design everything that Desconto needed, starting with the design of the card. Fortunately for me, they already had a nice logo. I designed the card, facebook graphics, leaflets, flyers, posters, booklets and flexes. I also designed and developed their website with two of my friends Praveen and Jeetu. In all, it was a great experience. This was the first time I was in charge of something this big and I thoroughly enjoyed working hard on it.
